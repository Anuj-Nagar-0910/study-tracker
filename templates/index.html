30-Day Statistics Boot-Camp for IIT Bombay’s AI/ML Diploma Screening

Main Takeaway:

Focus on **probability, statistics, linear algebra, basic calculus and core ML concepts** that IIT Bombay’s Centre for Machine Intelligence & Data Science (C-MInDS) lists in its admissions test syllabus. A disciplined 30-day, 2-3 h/day weekday plan built around high-quality open resources (ISLR, MIT OCW, Khan Academy, StatQuest) will give you the depth expected for the Statistical Foundations course and the online screening exam.

How the Screening Test Drives This Plan

IIT Bombay’s official syllabus for the MS/PhD & Diploma entrance test covers:

Area Key Topics (exam)
Probability & Statistics Descriptive stats, probability rules, random variables, estimation, regression
Calculus Differentiation, integration, partial derivatives
Linear Algebra Vectors, matrices, eigenvalues
Algorithms & Computing Asymptotic notation, basic data structures
ML Basics Over-fitting, gradient descent, classification/regression intuition

The ePG Diploma curriculum then deepens these themes in “Statistical Foundations of ML” and “Programming for ML & DS”. Your 30-day plan reflects exactly these layers.
